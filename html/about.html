About Us

2007

The Founding of Vesh

Vesh was founded in 2007, by a group of visionary dairy farmers in rural Singapore. Their mission was to provide the local community with fresh, high-quality milk, while promoting sustainable farming practices and supporting local livelihoods. The company's founding days were marked by hard work, dedication, and a commitment to excellence. The farmers rose with the sun to tend to their cows, carefully milking and bottling the creamy liquid to ensure its freshness and purity.

2015-2017

The Rise of Vesh

As the company grew, so did its reputation. Vesh begin supplying milk to cafes, restaurants and household across the region. The company's commitment to quality and sustainability earned it a loyal customer base. In 2015, Vesh introduced its first pasteurization and bottling plant, increasing efficiency and capacity while maintaining the highest standards of quality and safety. In 2017, Vesh launched its first organic milk product line, catering to the growing demand for natural and sustainable food options.

Today

Today, Vesh is a leading dairy company globally, renowned for its high-quality milk products and sustainable practices. The company product range has expanded to include a variety of milk types all made with the finest ingredients and produced with the utmost care. With a strong presence in the market, Vesh operates multiple dairy farms, and maintains a robust distribution network, making its products available in major retailers, restaurants, and cafes across the world.
