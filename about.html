About Us

2007

The Founding of Vesh

Vesh was founded in 2007, by a group of visionary dairy farmers in rural Singapore. Their mission was to provide the local community with fresh, high-quality milk, while promoting sustainable farming practices and supporting local livelihoods. The company's founding days were marked by hard work, dedication, and a commitment to excellence. The farmers rose with the sun to tend to their cows, carefully milking and bottling the creamy liquid to ensure its freshness and purity.

2015-2017

The Rise of Vesh

As the company grew, so did its reputation. Vesh begin supplying milk to cafes, restaurants and household across the region. The company's commitment to quality, sustainability, and community earned it a loyal customer base and regonition within the industry. In 2015, Vesh introduced its first pasteurization and bottling plant, increasing efficiency and capacity while maintaining the highest standards of quality and safety. This investment enabled the company to expand its distribution network, making its products more widely available to consumers. In 2017, Vesh launched its first organic milk product line, catering to the growing demand for natural and sustainable food options. This move not only expanded the company's product portfolio but also reinforced its commitment to environmentally friendly practices and sustainable agriculture.

Today

Today, Vesh is a leading dairy company globally, renowned for its high-quality milk products, sustainable practices and community engagement. The company product range has expanded to include a variety of milk types all made with the finest ingredients and produced with the utmost care. With a strong presence in the market, Vesh operates multiple dairy farms, and maintains a robust distribution network, making its products available in major retailers, restaurants, and cafes across the world. Vesh remains committed to its founding principles, prioritizing the welfare of cows, the environment, and the community. Vesh continues to invest in sustainable practices, such as solar-powered farms to minimize its environmental footprint. This commitment to sustainability and social responsibility has remained at the heart of Green Pastures Milk Company's mission, guiding its growth and success for generations to come.
